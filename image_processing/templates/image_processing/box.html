How to Use the Box Blur Operation

Follow these simple steps to apply a Box Blur to your image:

  1. Click on the "Browse" button to upload an image from your device.
  2. In the "Mask Size" field, select the desired mask size for the blur effect.
  3. Once you've chosen your options, click "Apply Box Blur" to process the image.
  4. Wait a few moments, and the blurred image will be displayed.

If you're not satisfied with the result, feel free to adjust the mask size and reapply the operation.
