How to Use Human Detection

To detect humans in your uploaded image, follow these steps:

  1. Click on "Browse" to upload an image.
  2. Click "Apply Human Detection" to start the process.
  3. Wait a few moments, and any detected humans will be highlighted with bounding boxes.

The detection algorithm only detects humans with more than 50% confidence.
