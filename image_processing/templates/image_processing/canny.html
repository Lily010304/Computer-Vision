How to Use the Canny Edge Detector

Follow these steps to detect edges in your image using the Canny Edge Detector:

  1. Click on the "Browse" button to upload an image.
  2. Once you've uploaded the image, click "Apply Canny" to start the edge detection process.
  3. Wait a few moments, and the processed image with detected edges will be displayed.

For better results, ensure the image has clear contrast between different areas.
