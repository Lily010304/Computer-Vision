How to Stitch Images Together

To stitch multiple images, follow these steps:

  1. Click on "Browse" to select multiple images (hold CTRL or SHIFT to choose more than one).
  2. Click "Upload Images" to begin stitching.
  3. The stitched image will be displayed once the process completes.

Ensure that the images have similar perspectives and overlapping content for best results.
