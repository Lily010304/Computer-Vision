How to Apply Smoothing

To apply smoothing to your image, follow these steps:

  1. Click "Browse" to upload your image.
  2. Select the smoothing parameter (e.g., mask size) using the available options.
  3. Click "Apply Smoothing" to process the image.
  4. The smoothed image will be displayed after processing.

Adjust the mask size for finer control over the smoothing effect.
